First things first, Rules

  • As usual, one picture for each prompt.
  • Any camera is allowed this time, whatever you're comfortable with you can use. If it captures light and can be translated to a digital format, it counts
  • Because of our unique judges this time around, you are no longer constrained by the time at which the picture was taken. As long as you were the individual to take the picture and you think it fits the category, it's a valid entry.
  • Once you're satisfied with your picture submissions, please email them to me by, at the latest Nov 26th in a zip file with each picture labeled as its corresponding number in the prompt list.
  • This should not have to be mentioned but for clarity: You have to physically take the picture. No AI and no post processing should be done to modify or create the images.

The main event: The Prompt List

  1. Aquatic (Fish, body of water, water feature, your sink, whatever)
  2. Picture of words (Think street sign, newspaper, textbook)
  3. Fridge picture (I wrote this down last year and I have no clue what it means, intrepret how you want)
  4. Trash (Do I mean a trash quality picture or trash subject? Up to the judges)
  5. A Pattern or A Fractal or something geometric (Anything that is a pattern, a fractal, or entirely geometric)
  6. Sparkles, Glowing, Luminscence (Anything that sparkles, glows, or is luminescent)
  7. Freespace (Anything PG-13)
  8. A boring picture (A well taken picture, just of boring content)


Finally, judging rules

Before each judge, should be a score card and pen. For each prompt there will be 5 pictures and each judge will rank them from best to worst (5-1) how they see fit. After each judge has ranked each picture of a prompt set and written down their scores we will identify each participants picture and distribute points. The participant with the GREATEST number of points, will win the contest and be awarded their trophy.
